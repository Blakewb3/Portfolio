About Me

Hello, I'm Blake Wallace. I am an aspiring, soon to be software developer. In December of 2023 I will have graduated from the University of Denver with a full stack developer certifaction,
and the skills and integrity to go along with it. Thank you for checking out my portfolio and I hope you enjoy it as much as I enjoyed building it.

Since I was young I always had a strong interest in seeing how things worked and how they were made. I remember in my early years I would be more excited to take apart a moving
toy then to actually play with it just to see the moving parts. Later in my teenage years I started being very interested in technology. It started by building my own computer and then I
had an amazing opportunity to work for TTM technologies in my senior year of high school where I learned how to build circuit boards.

Apart from being interested in hardware, I found myself getting progressively more intrigued about software and the overall user interface of the technology we all use everyday. I really enjoy
that you can build something out of nothing, and be as creative as you want to be. I extremly look forward to seeing where this adventure and carrer will take me, and cant wait to build somthing
amazing thaty people can incorperate in the day to day lives.
